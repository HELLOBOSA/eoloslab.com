Curated services · Four, by design

What the International Desk does. And what it deliberately doesn't.

S / 01

Brand Identity & Strategy.

Naming, positioning, the visual and verbal system. Designed to outlive a launch and to be useful to whoever ships next.
  • Positioning & naming
  • Identity system + design language
  • Verbal identity, voice, copy frameworks
  • Brand guidelines built for use, not display
S / 02

Web Design & Build.

Sites that sell what the brand already promises. Hand-built. Custom CMS. No templates, no theatre, no kit you'll outgrow in a year.
  • Custom design + development
  • Editorial CMS (your team, not ours)
  • Performance & SEO foundations
  • E-commerce when it earns its place
S / 03

Creative Direction.

Long-form partnership. The taste, the standard, the brief behind every campaign and every season.
  • Campaign + seasonal direction
  • Art direction for photo & film
  • Launch & positioning narrative
  • Quarterly review cadence
S / 04

Visualization — by application.

For real estate, hospitality and retail spaces we believe in. Photoreal interiors and exteriors, materials decided before the build.
  • Photoreal interior + exterior CGI
  • Day / dusk / night sequences
  • Material & finish decision board
  • Pre-sale & marketing rollout
A short list of things
we don't do here

We don't run pitches. We don't write proposals to win the work. We have a free discovery call and decide together if we're the right fit.

Print, packaging supply chain, full ad-agency production, signage manufacture — handled by the local studio that owns your market, not the desk.

How we engage

Four steps. Two on-sites, the rest where the work happens.

01 · Discovery

Discovery call

A free 30-minute call. The partner who'd run your project. We agree what to build and whether we should.

02 · Audit

Strategy & audit

Competitor, customer, current-state. A written position before a single shape is drawn.

03 · Design

Make the thing

Identity, web, direction. Weekly working sessions in your time zone. One round of strategic review per phase.

04 · Land

Launch & live

We ship. We stay 90 days. Then either we keep going on retainer or we hand it cleanly to your team.

Why this exists

Most of the work that crosses borders is done badly because nobody owns it end-to-end. The International Desk exists so one partner does.

You don't need a studio in your city. You need a studio that has done the thing you're trying to do, in a market that looked a lot like yours, and is willing to fly twice to get it right.

Design is the lever. Everything else is the load.
